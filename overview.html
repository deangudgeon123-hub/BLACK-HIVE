Overview

BLACK-HIVE explores tactical swarm systems for security, large-venue monitoring, and critical response. This page summarizes the mission, scope, and near-term milestones.

  • Problem: blind spots and slow response in dynamic environments
  • Approach: coordinated multi-UAS autonomy with human-on-the-loop control
  • Outcomes: improved coverage, faster detection, safer escalation
